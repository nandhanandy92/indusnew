About us.

WELCOME TO
INDUS FASHIONS: Redefining Uniform Excellence Since 1998

At Indus Fashions, we take pride in being a dynamic team of young, innovative designers and expert manufacturers of high-quality uniforms. Over the years, we have catered to the unique needs of diverse industries and educational institutions, becoming a trusted name in the world of uniform manufacturing. Our motto is simple yet profound: "A uniform is not just an attire; it is an identity that boosts the image and self-confidence of the wearer." This guiding principle has fueled our journey, transforming us from humble beginnings into a one-stop solution for all school, college, and corporate uniform needs..

A Legacy of Quality and Innovation

Founded in 1998, Indus Fashions began its journey by directly marketing shirts and trousers on the bustling streets of Chennai. Through unwavering commitment, exceptional craftsmanship, and a customer-centric approach, we have grown into a brand synonymous with trust, quality, and innovation in the uniform industry.



Our Process

Right from the conceptualization, designing and production of the uniforms, at all stages of the processes involved, we always strive to keep the cost at a minimum without compromising the quality. Our experienced staff and the skilled labourers are also committed to our ideologies and thus, besides maintaining the cost and quality, we also assure our clients prompt and timely delivery of the orders without deviation.

Our Vision

Our Vision is to be the preferred global partner for quality uniform fabrics. We envision a world where every uniform becomes a symbol of pride, reflecting the values and professionalism of the wearers. Through substainble practices & unwavering commitment, we aim to shape the future of uniform textiles

Our Mission

Our mission is to empower individuals and organizations with premium uniform fabrics that enhance comfort, confidence, and identity. We strive to be at the forefront of innovation, setting new benchmarks for equality and performance in the textile industry.

Our Products

School Uniforms, Notebooks, Embroidery, Sublimation Printing, Heat transfer Designs too are offered.
